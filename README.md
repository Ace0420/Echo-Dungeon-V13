# Echo Dungeon Version 13
## Blind First Edition

Making accessibility the norm in gaming.

---

## About

Echo Dungeon V13 is a voice-controlled dungeon crawler designed specifically for blind and visually impaired players. Navigate dungeons, battle monsters, collect treasure, and grow stronger using only your voice.

---

## Quick Start

1. Open the game in Chrome or Edge browser (HTTPS required)
2. Tap the screen to begin
3. Say "male", or "female" to choose pronouns
4. Say your race: human, elf, dwarf, orc, or halfling
5. Say your class: warrior, mage, or rogue
6. Say "save game as [your name]" to save your character
7. Start exploring!

## Character Creation

### Genders and Pronouns
- Male: he/him/his
- Female: she/her/hers

### Races

**Human**
- Balanced and versatile
- No attribute bonuses

**Elf**
- Graceful and wise
- +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom, -1 Strength

**Dwarf**
- Strong and resilient
- +3 Strength, -1 Dexterity

**Orc**
- Powerful but savage
- +4 Strength, -2 Wisdom

**Halfling**
- Lucky and agile
- +3 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom, -2 Strength
- Special: Lucky Break (+15% dodge chance)

### Classes

**Warrior**
- High health, uses physical attacks
- Special: Shield Bash (stuns enemy, adds shield defense to damage)
- Base stats: 10 STR, 5 DEX, 5 WIS

**Mage**
- High mana, uses powerful spells
- Special: Fireball (devastating fire damage)
- Base stats: 5 STR, 5 DEX, 10 WIS

**Rogue**
- Balanced, uses stealth and poison
- Special: Backstab (high damage sneak attack)
- Base stats: 5 STR, 10 DEX, 5 WIS

## Attributes

### Strength (STR)
- +5 max health per point
- Warriors: +2 damage per point
- Others: +1 damage per point

### Dexterity (DEX)
- +1% critical hit chance per point (max 50%)
- +0.5% dodge chance per point (max 25%)
- Rogues: +1 damage per point

### Wisdom (WIS)
- +5 max mana per point
- +3% spell damage per point
- -0.5% mana cost per point (max 50% reduction)

### Level Up
- Gain 3 attribute points per level
- Say "strength", "dexterity", or "wisdom" to allocate points

---

## Combat System

### Critical Hits
- Based on Dexterity
- 1% chance per DEX point
- Deals double damage
- Maximum 50% crit chance

### Dodge
- Based on Dexterity
- 0.5% chance per DEX point
- Completely avoid enemy attacks
- Maximum 25% dodge chance
- Halflings get +15% bonus

### Ring of Regeneration
- Heals 50 HP per ring per turn
- Unlimited stacking allowed
- Auto-equips when purchased
- Healing happens before enemy attacks

---

## Equipment System

### Weapon Slots
- 1 weapon (affects attack power)

### Armor Slots
- 1 armor
- 1 shield (warriors only)
- 1 helmet
- 1 gloves
- 1 boots

### Accessory Slots
- Up to 10 rings
- Up to 10 bracelets
- 1 amulet

### Auto-Equip
All equipment purchased from merchants auto-equips when possible.

---

## Special Features

### Junk Bag System
- Mark unwanted equipment for sale
- Say "add to junk [item name]"
- Sell all junk at once to merchants
- Get 60% of item value

### Merchants
- Found randomly in dungeon rooms
- Buy potions, equipment, and ability books
- Sell your junk for gold

### Special Rooms

**Shrines**
- Random blessings
- Permanent stat bonuses or gold

**Fountains**
- Restore 50% health and mana
- One use per fountain

**Libraries**
- Find ability books
- Learn new spells and abilities

### Floor Multipliers
Every 10 floors:
- Damage multiplier doubles
- Defense multiplier doubles
- Mana costs reduced

---

## Save System

### Save Your Character
- Say "save game as [your name]"
- Example: "save game as Gandalf"
- Creates a named save slot

### Load Your Character
- Say "load game [name]"
- Example: "load game Gandalf"

### View Characters
- Say "my characters"
- Lists all saved heroes

### Auto-Save
- Game auto-saves every 5 rooms explored

---

## Tips for Success

1. Search every room for hidden treasures
2. Equip better gear as you find it
3. Save potions for tough boss fights
4. Buy Rings of Regeneration from merchants
5. Stack multiple Rings of Regeneration for massive healing
6. Learn new abilities from books
7. Allocate attribute points wisely
8. Use the junk bag to sell unwanted gear
9. Visit shrines for permanent bonuses
10. Deeper levels have stronger enemies but better rewards
